The Five Pillars of Hegemonic Power

Neo-Americanism operates across five interconnected dimensions of power, forming a self-reinforcing system that extends far beyond traditional military and economic dominance.

1

Technological Hegemony

Innovation-capital-market spiral, frontier AI/quantum/semiconductor leadership, and global talent siphoning.

2

Military Hegemony

All-domain intelligent capabilities, global alliance architecture, and low-cost offshore balancing strategies.

3

Cultural & Lifestyle Hegemony

Four-tier penetration from sensory experience to identity internalization, amplified by digital platforms.

4

Institutional Hegemony

Material, institutional, and cognitive structural power, transitioning from global to minilateral bloc governance.

5

Economic Hegemony

Dollar reserve status, high-end global value chain monopoly, and transnational tech capital power.

Three Future Scenarios to 2050

Plausible trajectories for the evolution of global hegemonic order, based on current structural trends and critical uncertainties.

Scenario A

Hegemonic Perpetuation

U.S. achieves breakthroughs in AGI and fault-tolerant quantum computing, creating a generational technological gap that re-establishes unipolar technological dominance.

Tech unipolarity re-established
Center-periphery global structure
